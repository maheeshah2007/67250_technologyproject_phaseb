About MonoMuse

Mission

MonoMuse is dedicated to exploring the evolving relationship between art, technology, and society. Our mission is to inspire curiosity and creativity by showcasing innovative exhibitions that highlight how digital tools, emerging technologies, and human imagination shape the future of artistic expression.

Who We Serve

MonoMuse welcomes visitors of all ages and backgrounds — from students and educators to artists, technologists, and lifelong learners. We are committed to making contemporary art accessible and meaningful for everyone.

Opening Hours

Tuesday – Sunday, 10am – 6pm. Closed Mondays and public holidays.

About Us

Founded in 2016, MonoMuse has grown from a single-gallery space into a leading institution at the intersection of art and digital innovation. We partner with artists, technologists, and universities to bring bold new ideas to life.

Milestones
  • 2023: Expansion of the museum space and launch of international artist collaborations.
  • 2018: Launch of the Interactive Media Lab, allowing visitors to experiment with creative coding.
  • 2016: MonoMuse opens its doors with its first exhibition on digital storytelling.
